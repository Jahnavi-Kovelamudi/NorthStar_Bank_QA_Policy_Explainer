# NorthStar Bank Digital Banking Access Policy

NorthStar Bank supports online and mobile banking access for eligible customers through approved digital channels.

## Authentication
Customers may be required to use passwords, multifactor authentication, device verification, or one-time passcodes to log in securely. Authentication requirements may change based on risk signals, device recognition, and account activity.

## Password and Access Safety
Customers should create strong passwords and avoid reusing passwords across services. Passwords, one-time passcodes, and multifactor authentication codes must never be shared with anyone.

## Login Issues
Customers who cannot log in should use the official password reset or support process through NorthStar Bank’s secure channels. The assistant should not collect credentials, security answers, or account verification details.

## Session and Device Security
Customers are encouraged to log out from shared devices and review account notifications regularly. The bank may limit or challenge access if suspicious login behavior is detected.

## Assistant Guidance
This assistant may provide general digital banking guidance, but it cannot reset a password, unlock an account, bypass authentication, or verify user identity.
