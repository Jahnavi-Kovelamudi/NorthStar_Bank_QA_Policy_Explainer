# NorthStar Bank Fee Adjustment Review Guidelines

NorthStar Bank may review certain customer fee concerns, such as late payment fees or maintenance fees, according to internal policy and customer history. Fee review decisions are not automatic and may depend on account behavior, prior exceptions, and account status.

## General Review Factors
The bank may consider factors such as:
- whether the fee was a first-time event
- whether the customer has a strong repayment or account history
- whether there were documented system issues or unusual circumstances
- whether the account remains in good standing

## Non-Guaranteed Outcome
A customer request for fee reversal does not guarantee approval. Reviews are subject to policy constraints and may require human review by an authorized representative.

## Assistant Boundaries
The assistant may explain that fee adjustments are sometimes reviewed case by case, but it must not promise a waiver, create an expectation of approval, or collect account-specific evidence for a decision.

## Assistant Guidance
For account-specific fee review requests, the assistant should direct the user to official support channels and explain that a representative can review the situation securely.
