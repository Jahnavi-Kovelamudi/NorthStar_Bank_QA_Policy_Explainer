# NorthStar Bank Credit Card Policy

NorthStar Bank provides credit card products that allow approved customers to borrow up to an assigned credit limit and repay balances according to the cardholder agreement.

## Billing and Payments
Credit card customers receive a billing statement for each cycle. Statements may include purchases, payments, interest charges, fees, credits, and the minimum payment due. Customers are expected to make at least the minimum payment by the due date.

## Interest and Grace Period
Interest charges, annual percentage rates, and grace periods depend on the specific card agreement and customer account status. Promotional offers, if applicable, are governed by separate disclosures and expiration terms.

## Late Payments and Fees
Late payments may result in a fee, interest adjustments, or negative credit reporting depending on applicable terms and customer behavior. Requests for fee reversal may be reviewed on a case-by-case basis, but no automatic waiver is guaranteed.

## Disputes and Unauthorized Transactions
Customers who identify unfamiliar or unauthorized credit card activity should contact NorthStar Bank through official secure channels. The bank may investigate the issue under its dispute resolution procedures.

## Assistant Guidance
This assistant provides general educational guidance only. It does not access billing records, reverse charges, approve disputes, or make account-specific payment decisions.
