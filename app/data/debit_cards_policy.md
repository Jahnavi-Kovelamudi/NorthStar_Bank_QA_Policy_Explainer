# NorthStar Bank Debit Card Policy

NorthStar Bank issues debit cards linked to eligible checking accounts. Debit cards allow customers to access funds in their account through purchases, ATM withdrawals, and certain digital wallet transactions.

## Card Usage
Debit card transactions are deducted directly from the linked account balance. Customers are responsible for reviewing transaction activity regularly and reporting any suspicious or unauthorized activity promptly.

## Lost or Stolen Cards
If a customer believes a debit card has been lost, stolen, or used without authorization, the customer should contact NorthStar Bank immediately through official support channels. The bank may block the card and issue a replacement based on its verification procedures.

## ATM and Daily Limits
ATM withdrawal limits and daily purchase limits may apply for security reasons. These limits vary by card type and customer profile. Exact limit values are not provided by this assistant because they may depend on account settings and internal controls.

## Assistant Guidance
This assistant may explain general debit card policies and safety guidance, but it cannot freeze a card, replace a card, change limits, or verify account-specific transactions.
