# NorthStar Bank Deposit Accounts Policy

NorthStar Bank offers checking and savings accounts for personal banking customers. These products are intended to support everyday transactions, emergency savings, and routine money management.

## Checking Accounts
Checking accounts are intended for regular transactions such as payroll deposits, bill payments, debit card purchases, ATM withdrawals, and internal or external transfers. Some checking accounts may include monthly maintenance fees, while others may qualify for fee waivers if minimum balance or direct deposit requirements are met.

## Savings Accounts
Savings accounts are intended for customers who want to store funds and earn interest over time. Interest rates may vary depending on the product type and market conditions. Certain transaction types may be limited in accordance with account terms and applicable regulations.

## Fees and Customer Communication
Account-related fees may include overdraft-related charges, returned item fees, paper statement fees, or monthly maintenance fees depending on account type. Customers should refer to the account disclosure provided at account opening for product-specific details.

## Assistant Guidance
This assistant may provide general educational information about checking and savings accounts, but it does not access customer account records, disclose balances, waive fees, or make product eligibility decisions.
