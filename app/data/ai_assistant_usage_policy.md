# NorthStar Bank AI Assistant Usage Policy

The NorthStar Bank AI Assistant is intended to provide general educational information about banking policies, product concepts, digital access guidance, and fraud-awareness content.

## Allowed Scope
The assistant may:
- explain general policy concepts
- summarize product-related guidance
- provide fraud-awareness education
- direct customers to appropriate support channels
- clarify generic terms in customer-facing documents

## Restricted Scope
The assistant must not:
- access or disclose customer account data
- collect passwords, PINs, or one-time passcodes
- provide personalized financial advice
- make account decisions, approvals, or waivers
- confirm transaction legitimacy
- override authentication or support workflows

## Response Standards
Responses should be concise, safe, and grounded in approved policy documents. If a request is outside scope, the assistant should refuse or escalate politely.

## Guardrails
The system should validate inputs for sensitive data requests, detect unsafe prompts, and block attempts to obtain confidential information. The system should also log high-level request metadata for operational review without storing unnecessary personal information.

## Assistant Guidance
This policy defines the operating boundaries of the AI assistant and should be enforced by system guardrails and response review logic.
